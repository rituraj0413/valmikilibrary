Valmiki Library Mentorship

Create your mentor profile

Build your verified mentor profile, share your job role and teaching strengths, set your daily or monthly pricing, and start taking online revision sessions with students.

What students will see

Your profile will show your role, experience, streams known, pricing, and the kind of preparation support you provide. Students can then request online mentorship sessions directly.

Portal flow

1. Create profile.

2. Verify your email.

3. Log in as mentor.

4. Add meeting links, questions, marks, and feedback for each student session.

Mentor Details

Verified mentors can manage student practice sessions and progress reports from their own dashboard.
